WHAT IS 100% FOCUS MOVEMENT


EXECUTIVE SUMMARY


100% FOCUS MOVEMENT is an independent self-sponsored social vehicle that supports potentially qualified political office seekers, and also helps with sensitization and providing support for campaign and support groups. After a scientific review of political parties and candidates, we settled to support APC and its Presidential candidate ASIWAJU BOLA TINUBU\KASHIM SHETTIMA, as well as all gubernatorial candidates and others.
We have successfully mobilized numerous grassroot support groups and conducted campaign projects in favour of ASIWAJU BOLA and all APC candidates in Lagos, Kaduna,Abuja,Bauchi,katsina,Kwara,and we will be heading to Kano,Jigawa,Oyo,Akwa-Ibom,Sokoto,Zamfara and Kebbi for the first batch,then other part of the country subsequently .
We have incubated and donated 3 main offices in Abuja(Northcentral),Bauchi(Northeast) and fully equipped Kaduna intelligence centre(Northwest) for use before ,during and after the 2023 general elections. Furthermore we are focused on enlightening groups and voters on the importance of , securing their PVC’S, voting TINUBU\SHETTIMA and delivering their individual polling units and defending it.




Our Activities & Programs

WHY WE CHOSE TINUBU/ SHETTIMA FOR BETTER AND UNITED NIGERIA – 100% Focus Movement

As Nigeria prepares for the 2023 General Elections in February and March 2023, a passionate group of Nigerians established a movement called 100% Focus Movement. After exhaustive assessment of all the candidates, their manifestoes and antecedents, 100% Focus Movement believe that Bola Tinubu has the capacity and experience to stabilize Nigeria and build on the achievements of President Mohammed Buhari. Bola Ahmed Adekunle Tinubu served as the Governor of Lagos State from 1999 to 2007. During his time as governor, he implemented a number of reforms and development projects that had a significant impact on the state. Some of his notable achievements as governor include:

Improving infrastructure: Tinubu oversaw the construction of new roads, bridges, and other infrastructure projects in Lagos, which helped to improve connectivity and boost economic development.

Increasing access to education and healthcare: Tinubu implemented a number of initiatives to improve access to education and healthcare in Lagos, including building new schools and healthcare facilities.

Reducing crime: Tinubu implemented a number of measures to reduce crime in Lagos, including increasing the number of police officers and improving their training and equipment.

Promoting economic development: Tinubu implemented a number of policies and initiatives to stimulate economic growth and development in Lagos, including attracting investment and supporting small businesses.

Enhancing environmental protection: Tinubu implemented a number of measures to protect the environment in Lagos, including promoting waste management and tree planting.

Education: Education was taken serious during Tinubu’s time as the governor of Lagos. He built Millennium Schools in different locations in the state. Not only that, students sitting for the SSCE Exam had their fees paid.
